Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 2026

Welcome to Bubblle.

Bubblle is a location-based anonymous chat platform that allows users to discover and participate in nearby conversations.

Information We Collect

Location Information

When you grant permission, Bubblle collects your approximate or precise location to help discover nearby chat bubbles and create location-based conversations.

Anonymous Session Information

We create an anonymous session identifier to help maintain your identity within the app without requiring account registration.

Messages and Content

Messages, bubble names, and other content you submit may be stored on our servers to provide the service.

Technical Information

We may collect technical information such as IP address, browser type, device information, and server logs for security, analytics, and service reliability.

How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

  • Provide nearby bubble discovery
  • Enable anonymous conversations
  • Improve platform performance
  • Prevent abuse and spam
  • Maintain security and reliability

Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information.

We may disclose information if required by law, legal process, or to protect the safety, rights, and security of users or the platform.

Data Retention

We retain information only as long as reasonably necessary to operate, improve, and secure the service.

Expired bubbles and related content may be automatically removed.

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect user information, but no internet service can guarantee absolute security.

Children's Privacy

Bubblle is not intended for children under 13 years of age.

Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the service constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
